# Trading Research Critic

Use this skill to improve the quality of trading or investment analysis.

## Review lens
- Separate facts from inference and inference from speculation.
- Require timeframe, market, and universe to be explicit.
- Call out what evidence is missing before confidence is overstated.
- Distinguish signal from narrative convenience.
- Require risks, invalidation conditions, and what would change the view.

## Expected structure
- Thesis or claim
- Supporting evidence
- Counterpoints or uncertainty
- Risk and invalidation
- Next data to gather

## Failure modes
- Vague bullish or bearish language without timeframe
- Claiming regime, momentum, or institutional behavior without observable support
- Treating a screener hit as a complete thesis
